This memo outlines the complete brand identity for SHARPEST — an AI automation agency built on a single philosophy: no fluff, just results. Use this document as your primary reference for all design decisions. When in doubt, ask yourself: is this sharp enough?

01 Origin & Story
The story

"A friend always said about people who weren't quite getting it — 'he's not the sharpest pencil in the box.' We built AI agents that are."

SHARPEST is a Czech-founded AI automation agency. The name comes from a well-known saying about intelligence and wit — turned into a mission. We are the sharpest tool in your business. The brand should feel like it was built by someone who has been inside broken systems, seen the inefficiency up close, and decided to fix it. No drama. Just work.

The founder is a lawyer who thinks like an engineer. That combination — precision + systems thinking — should be visible in every brand touchpoint.

03 Visual Direction — Neo Brutalism

The visual style is neo brutalism — raw, intentional, high contrast. No rounded corners. No gradients. No shadows. No stock photography. Everything is deliberate. The design should feel like it was built by someone who cares deeply about precision and has zero patience for decoration that doesn't serve a purpose.

Grid
Strict. Rigid.
Everything aligned. No floating elements.
Borders
2px solid black.
Sharp edges everywhere. No border-radius.
Whitespace
Generous but precise.
Breathing room that feels intentional, not lazy.

References: Linear.app, Vercel, early Figma, Gumroad redesign. But colder. More Central European. Less Silicon Valley.

04 Logo

The logo is purely typographic. No icon. No pencil. No abstract mark. The word SHARPEST is the logo. Set in a bold geometric sans-serif or display typeface. Always uppercase. The name is strong enough to stand alone.

07 Voice & Tone
WE WRITE LIKE THIS
  • Short sentences. No filler. Every word earns its place.
  • "We automate the work you hate." Direct. Personal.
  • "Results in 30 days." Specific. Measurable.
  • "Sharp. Fast. Done." Rhythm matters.
  • Dry humour when appropriate. Never forced.
WE NEVER WRITE
  • "Revolutionary AI-powered solutions"
  • "We leverage cutting-edge technology to..."
  • "Game-changing" / "World-class" / "Best-in-class"
  • Passive voice
  • Long paragraphs that could be one sentence

10 What SHARPEST Is Not

When in doubt — look at our competitors and do the opposite. Most AI agencies look identical: dark blue or black, hexagon patterns, stock photos of robots, vague promises. SHARPEST is none of that.

Not this
  • Rounded corners and friendly gradients
  • Blue color palette — "trustworthy tech"
  • Abstract robot or AI brain illustrations
  • Testimonials with stock headshots
  • Inter or Roboto as primary typeface
But this
  • Sharp edges, 2px borders, raw structure
  • Black, white, electric yellow — nothing else
  • Bold typography as the visual element
  • Real numbers, real results, real case studies
  • Bebas Neue + Instrument Sans + DM Mono
